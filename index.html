Who We Are

We are Sleeping Dragon Terrain! We own and run The Sleeping Dragon Hobby Shop, selling scenic modelling materials and hobby supplies to the wargaming and scale modelling community.

Sleeping Dragon Terrain is the little brother of the Hobby Shop, where we offer a commission service focussing on building and creating the very terrain and scenery pieces we sell products for.

What We Do

While we sell plenty of products already for folks who are happy making their own scenery and terrain, others don't always have the time, money or skills they might like to be able to make their own. That's where we come in.

We design, create and construct all elements of scenery and terrain for wargaming, roleplaying, railway modelling - you name it!

Whether you want a custom scenery piece making, a table's worth of MDF kits assembling and painting, a complete gaming table or anything in between, we can create whatever projects you have in mind.
